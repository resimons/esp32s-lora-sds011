## How to connect SDS011 to ESP32
SDS011 | COLOUR | ESP
------------ | ---------- | -------------
VCC | RED | 5,1V
GND | BLACK | GND
TX | BLUE | GPIO16
RX | GREEN | GPIO17

## Wiring LoRa SX1278 module with ESP32
SX1278 | COLOR | ESP32
-------- |-| ----------
VCC | RED | 3.3V
GND | BLACK | GND
SCK | ORANGE | GPIO18
MOSI | WHITE | GPIO23
DIO0 | BROWN | GPIO4
NSS | BLUE | GPIO5
MISO | GREEN | GPIO19
RST | YELLOW | GPIO2
